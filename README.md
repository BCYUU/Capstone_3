EmporiUm Capstone 3 

Overview

Project analyzes store sales in the Western region alongside Online sales across the country for EmporiUm.
Goal is to identify key performance drivers, regional and national trends, and growth opportunities.


Objective

Identify top-performing categories, states, store locations in the Western region, and leading online sales segments.  
Analyze revenue trends over time to detect growth patterns and seasonality.  
Build a dashboard to enable data exploration.
